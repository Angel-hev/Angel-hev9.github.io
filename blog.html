What I Love

I’ve always loved the adrenaline of playing volleyball — the same rush I feel when riding my motorcycle on open roads, especially during breathtaking sunsets that paint the sky with peace.

On quieter days, you’ll find me focused on a game of billiards, something I started because of my brother. It became a comforting escape and a fun challenge I truly enjoy.

I also join pageants — not just for the spotlight, but because I love embracing both grace and passion, whether it’s casual or competitive events.

But nothing beats those cozy moments with my family, sharing laughter and warm cups of coffee — my ultimate comfort drink, especially when I feel sad or overwhelmed.

I treasure every adventure with my boyfriend, Dale — whether we’re exploring new places or just being silly together. And I always look forward to the spontaneous hangouts with my best friend, Karlyn, whose energy lights up even the dullest day.

Life feels complete when it’s a perfect mix of sports, simple joys, and being with the people I love — even if it’s just another night playing billiards and making memories.
